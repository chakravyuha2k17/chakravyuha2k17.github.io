ABOUT CHAKRAVYUHA

CHAKRAVYUHA is the technical symposium conducted by Computer Science Department of Tagore Engineering College.

Paper Presentation

To all those upcoming and aspiring tech wizards. Here is an excellent opportunity awaiting you.

An opportunity that will put your interpersonal skills to test and explore various chances of presenting your paper of interesting geners. Stay tuned for the further updates on topics to be presented.



Paper Presentation Domain
1 - Networking
2 - Cloud Computing
3 - Big Data
4 - IOT(Internet of Things)
5 - AI(Artificial Intelligence)


Paper presentation Rules
1 - A team must comprise of a maximum of 2 members.

2 - The Paper Should not exceed 6 pages. 

3 - Teams must register themselves first to particiapte.

4 - Online Submission of paper should be done on or before 7.00 pm, 21st January 2017 at chakravyuha2k17@gmail.com mentioning the e-mail id and phone numbers of each participant.

5 - Participants will be given a total time of 15 minutes. (12 minutes for presentation, 3 minutes for queries.) 

6 - The Presentation should not exceed more than 15 slides. 

Code Untangle

Are you a programming brainbox?? Are you a person who can easily identify and rectify the errors?? Then this event is specially for you. Rounds have been set to put your programming abilities into various challenging tests.

First Round - Quiz
Questions from latest trends and technology.

Second Round - Debugging
Code will be given from following languages C, C++, Java, HTML, JAVASCRIPT and participants must debug it.

Third Round - Problem Solving
Simple problem statements will be given to the participants and they have to write the logic and execute it and show the output.

Chat Bot

This event is primarly an event where the participants will be shown a demo of doing a sample chat bot easily after which they will have to create a chat bot on the topic given onspot. For more info visit https://wit.ai/

Rupatra

The Ticking clock makes your nerves to tickle. Rupatra is a time managing event where three mind blowing games will make you excited and light the inner sprit in you.



Round 1
Gem Filling: There will be 4 coloured glasses placed in a certain order.The participants need to fill the glass with same coloured gems without changing the order in 1 minute.

Round 2
Hidden Object: Printout of an image will be given and a list of objects to be found. The participants will be selected based on the no of objects they found within 1 minute.

Round 3
Origami Unfold: The finalist will be given an origami model and a plain sheet .you have to find the origami pattern and should make a replica within 5 minutes.

Trinata

Trinata as the name suggests invites a team of three and comprises of three rounds that are very much familiar to you but yet challenging. Crack on each of them to win exciting prizes.

Round 1
Connections: the connection game is a type of abstract strategy game in which players attempt to complete specific type of connection with their pieces.
Round 2
Read on my fate: A team should have 3 members. After the clock begins to tick one of the player want to carry a word on his/her head then the opponents can give limited clues to point the word.
Round 3
Bouncing Ball: A team shud contain 3 players.within the time limit, the players want to tie up 4 glasses in their hands and legs to catch the bouncing ball.
