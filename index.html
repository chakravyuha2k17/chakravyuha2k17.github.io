ABOUT CHAKRAVYUHA

CHAKRAVYUHA is the technical symposium conducted by Computer Science Department of Tagore Engineering College.

Paper Presentation

Here is an amazing opportunity to showcase your ideas on an unique and massive platform in the form of well integrated and intriguing presentation. The stage has been set to listen to your words. Speak clearly,if you speak at all. All the best. Topics will be announced shortly.



Paper Presentation Domain
1 - Networking
2 - Cloud Computing
3 - Big Data
4 - IOT(Internet of Things)
5 - AI(Artificial Intelligence)


Paper presentation Rules
1 - The Paper Should not exceed 6 pages.

2 - Only Registered teams can submit the papers,online.

3 - Online Submission of paper should be done on or before 7.00 pm, 21st January 2017 at chakravyuha2k17@gmail.com mentioning the e-mail id and phone numbers of each participant.

4 - Participants will be given total timing of 15 minutes. 12 minutes for presentation, 3 minutes for queries.

5 - The Presentation may contain maximum of 15 slides.

Code Debugging

First Round - Quiz
Questions from latest trends and technology.

Second Round - Debugging
Code will be given from following languages C, C++, Java, HTML, JAVASCRIPT and participants must debug it.

Third Round - Problem Solving
Simple problem statements will be given to the participants and they have to write the logic and execute it and show the output.

Chat Bot

In this round, we give show a demo of how to do a sample chat bot in an easy way. After that Participants have to create a simple chatbot based on the topic we give at that time. For more Info Check Wit.ai

Rapsoflex

Rapsoflex has a three set of rounds
Round 1
Gem Filling: There will be 4 coloured glasses placed in a certain order.The participants need to fill the glass with same coloured gems without changing the order in 1 minute.
Round 2
Hidden Object: Printout of an image will be given and a list of objects to be found .The participants will be selected based on the no of objects they found within 1 minute.
Round 3
Origami: The finalist will be given an origami model and a plain sheet .you have to find the origami pattern and should make a replica within 5 minutes.

Trilathon

Trilathon has a three set of rounds
Round 1
Connections: A connection game is a type of abstract strategy game in which players attempt to complete a specific type of connection with their pieces
Round 2
Read on my fate: A team should have 3 members. After the clock begins to tick one of the player want to carry a word on his/her head then the opponents can give limited clues to point the word.
Round 3
Bouncing Ball: A team shud contain 3 players.within the time limit, the players want to tie up 4 glasses in their hands and legs to catch the bouncing ball.
